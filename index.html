About Me

Delivery leadership built for regulated, technical products.

I'm Johoney Bernardino, a Senior Technical Project Manager with 10+ years leading IT infrastructure, SaaS transformation, and managed service delivery for financial services and global enterprise clients. At Modulus Labs, I own end-to-end delivery of fintech payment products — from merchant terminal certification through production go-live — directing cross-functional teams across Product, Engineering, QA, Compliance, and payment partners.

My background spans Agile, Waterfall, and hybrid SDLC governance, having directed portfolios exceeding ₱128M in program value and 25+ concurrent transition programs. I combine disciplined project governance with hands-on risk management, vendor oversight, and stakeholder communication — translating technical complexity into clear decisions for executives and delivery teams alike.

I've also built AI-assisted workflows into how I plan, document, and report — using generative AI to accelerate drafting while keeping human judgment squarely in charge of quality, compliance, and accountability.
